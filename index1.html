Morphometric and Meristic Traits of Nemipteridae Species

This project visualizes and classifies Nemipteridae species using detailed morphological traits such as fin counts, body proportions, and meristic data. The dataset integrates taxonomic hierarchy and morphometric variations across species collected from various marine regions.

Explore the interactive notebook to analyze, visualize, and model taxonomic relationships using Neo4j-based graph taxonomy classification.

About the Dataset

The dataset includes species-specific morphometric and meristic traits such as standard length, head depth, snout length, fin ray counts, gill rakers, and other proportional measurements. Each record is tagged with its corresponding region or sampling area for comparative taxonomy analysis.
